## Problem

Many teams are experimenting with AI coding assistants, but the bottleneck often appears before code starts. Requirements may be incomplete, permissions may be undefined, platform dependencies may be hidden, and release risk may appear too late.

## Solution

Use confidence gates before build and promotion.

The model is simple:

1. Score the Jira requirement.
2. Ask Product or the assigned developer targeted questions when confidence is low.
3. Attempt a sandbox build only when readiness is high enough.
4. Validate code, platform dependencies, permissions, and tests.
5. Hand developers evidence, not guesses.
6. Require human authorization before promotion to the next release region.

## Positioning Line

The next step in engineering is not autonomous code shipping. It is agent assisted delivery with human controlled promotion.
